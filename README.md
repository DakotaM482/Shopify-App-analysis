Analyst Memo — Shopify App Store Insights
Dashboard: Shopify App Store Analysis
Reporting Period: Latest available data
Key Insight
The timing of when a business decides to release their app is one of the most crucial decisions to make according to the data.
Business Impact
There are key months where the average review is significantly lower than other months much more than when comparing year to year. This might be based on how many apps are released or many other factors.
Recommendation
Based on the data I would personally recommend releasing your app in April. April has a spike in average reviews of 4.25 which is lower than November however, the second quarter is the best performing as quarter with an average score of 4.21 compared to quarters 4's average of 4.18.
